# Managing your favorites

Marking passwords as favorites is easy:

1.  While logged in, click “passwords“ in the upper left.
2.  Click on the star next to the password(s) you want to favorite. The star will turn red.
3.  Click on “Favorites“ on the left menu (beyond “All items“) to see the password(s) you have marked.
4.  Just click the star again to remove a password from your favorites list and the star will become grey.
